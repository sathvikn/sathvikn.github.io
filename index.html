Sathvik Nair

Hello! I'm a third year undergraduate at UC Berkeley double-majoring in Computer Science and Cognitive Science. This semester, I'm an undergraduate student instructor for UC Berkeley's introductory data science course (Data 8), and working as a research assistant at the International Computer Science Institute on the FrameNet project. In addition to teaching, I have experience with fullstack development and data analysis through both research and the tech industry.

My interests include the application of data science techniques, specifically natural language processing, to research in the behavioral sciences, as well as using technology to democratize various services and products for a more equitable world. I also enjoy writing about topics like technology's impact on the world and South Asian American identity.

In my free time, I like to read books and articles from economics to contemporary fiction, play the violin and percussion(and listen to almost any kind of music), and cook new recipes.
