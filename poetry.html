Poetry

I began writing poetry after my junior year of high school. Although I began writing for leisure through writing stories, I initially strayed away from poetry. However, when I tried writing poems during my AP English class, I couldn't help but continue. Given my musical background, I'm intrigued by how language can be manipulated to create sound and rhythm. I've gone on to describe my personal experiences, certain events, and interact with my culture through poetry.

Many of these poems are shaped by my experience as a student and were written whenever I felt a moment or experience should be rendered into verse.

  • Mandatory Reading, Optional Chairs (Reading on the grass)
  • Satisfaction from Studying
  • Reflection through Exertion (Why I run)
  • Unburied Treasure (Cleaning out my room before college)
  • Go Bears (How I chose to attend Berkeley)
  • Fickle (On the college admissions process)
